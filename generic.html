Robotics

Our robotics program is based upon the Vex Robotics Competitive Program , and we participate in the Create Foundation Division.

We have up to 2 teams and each team has 3 main positions. We have builders, drivers, and programmers! Each team is responsible for designing and then creating their robot to take to competitions.

Competitions!
We are a competitive club and got to between 3 and 5 competitions each year. Points are awarded upon performance and further competitions are able to be reached even so far as international!
